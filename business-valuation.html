Why We Don't Believe in Business Valuation Calculators

While many websites offer "instant business valuation calculators," we believe they do more harm than good. Calculators use general assumptions instead of considering all the non-math insights that dramatically impact business value.

Critical factors calculators miss:

  • Strength and depth of the management team
  • Long-serving employee base and institutional knowledge
  • Current and forecasted market conditions
  • Business liquidity and cash flow timing
  • Debt structure and financial obligations
  • Customer concentration and contract terms
  • Competitive positioning and market barriers
  • Industry-specific risks and opportunities

The Real Cost of Calculator Valuations

Business owners who rely on calculator estimates often enter negotiations with unrealistic expectations, leading to failed deals, wasted time, and missed opportunities. Professional business valuation reveals the real story behind the numbers.

The Six-Year Sabbatical That Brought Clarity

After completing the Venezuela-China transaction in 2016, a deal that tested every skill I'd developed over three decades. I did something unexpected. I retired.

My wife and I bought a trawler and spent six years cruising to the Caribbean. ( You can read about the adventure and see pictures of the trip at: LifeOnKokomo.com ) Those years on the water gave me time to reflect on hundreds of deals, thousands of negotiations, and the patterns that determine success or failure in business transactions.

"Somewhere between the Bahamas and Grenada, I realized that in every deal I'd done, whether it succeeded or failed the crucial factor was always the same: accurate valuation at the right time."

When I returned in 2022 and rekindled my consulting practice, I knew exactly where to focus. Business owners needed someone who had not only studied valuations but had lived through the consequences of both accurate and inaccurate ones, or none at all. Someone who understood that behind every valuation is a human story, a family's future, a life's work.
